# ERP Simulation — SAP S/4HANA

**Category:** End-to-End Supply Chain · Manufacturing Operations
**Scope:** 15 Products · Full production and logistics cycle
**Outcome:** 33% improvement in simulated profitability

---

## Overview

Simulated end-to-end manufacturing and supply chain operations for 15 products using SAP S/4HANA. The project applied production planning, capacity management, and data-driven optimization to improve operational and financial performance across a simulated enterprise environment.

---

## What I Did

**Operations Simulation**
- Set up production planning, labor routings, capacity planning, and cost estimating workflows in SAP ERP for 15 distinct products
- Simulated procurement, production scheduling, inventory control, and logistics operations end-to-end
- Applied demand planning and forecasting to balance supply against simulated demand signals

**Data Analysis & Optimization**
- Applied SQL-based data analysis to identify bottlenecks, excess inventory, and demand variability
- Optimized demand planning and distribution logistics to reduce cost and improve delivery performance
- Applied Lean principles and waste elimination to streamline simulated production flows

**Financial Impact**
- Measured profitability impact of process improvements against baseline simulation
- Achieved **33% improvement in simulated profitability** through combined operational changes

## Result

> 📈 33% improvement in simulated profitability
