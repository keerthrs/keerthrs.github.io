# ATV Suspension System — SAE Baja

**Category:** Full Product Design Lifecycle · Cross-functional Team Leadership
**Role:** Vice Leader & Lead Designer
**Outcome:** Top 10% among 100+ international teams

---

## Overview

Led the full-cycle detailed mechanical design of an all-terrain vehicle (ATV) suspension system as Vice Leader of the SAE Baja team. This project covered every stage from concept generation through system-level competition validation.

---

## What I Did

**Design & Analysis**
- Designed all suspension components and sub-assemblies in SolidWorks 3D CAD with full 2D engineering drawing packages
- Performed tolerance stack-up analysis across all mating interfaces to ensure assembly fit and function
- Conducted FEA-informed structural analysis — evaluated load paths, stress distribution, and deflection under competition loading conditions
- Applied PFMEA at the component and system level to identify and mitigate high-risk failure modes before build

**Prototyping & Validation**
- Built prototypes using CNC machining, 3D printing, and fabrication processes
- Conducted hands-on testing and iterated rapidly on designs based on test results
- Applied DFM/DFA principles to drive designs toward mass production feasibility
- Selected materials based on load, weight, cost, and machinability requirements

**Team Leadership**
- Directed a cross-functional team of engineers through structured design reviews
- Managed project scheduling, resource allocation, and budgeting under strict deadlines
- Presented technical findings and design decisions to competition judges
