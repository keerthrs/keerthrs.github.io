# Battery Thermal Management System

**Category:** IoT-Based · EV Application · Electromechanical Systems
**Outcome:** 20% improvement in projected battery lifecycle

---

## Overview

Designed an IoT-based electromechanical thermal management system targeting EV battery packs. The project integrated hardware design, thermal analysis, and supplier capability assessment into a single validated system architecture.

---

## What I Did

**System Design**
- Integrated sensors, actuators, and electrical components into a compact miniaturized architecture
- Developed 3D CAD layouts and component specifications for all mechanical sub-assemblies
- Defined system architecture targeting world-class thermal performance standards

**Engineering Analysis**
- Performed FEA-informed heat transfer analysis to model thermal behavior under operating and edge-case conditions
- Validated design performance against functional requirements for temperature uniformity, response time, and lifecycle targets
- Assessed supplier capability for sourced components — reviewed dimensional conformance and process stability

**Outcome**
- Achieved a projected **20% improvement in battery lifecycle** through controlled thermal management

## Result

> ⚡ 20% improvement in projected battery lifecycle
