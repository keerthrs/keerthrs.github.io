# ISO 9001:2015 & ISO 14001:2015 Internal Audit

**Category:** Quality Management System · Environmental Management System
**Type:** Academic Project — Full audit cycle using real-world methodology
**Outcome:** Complete auditable findings report with CAR documentation

---

## Overview

Planned and executed process-based internal audits for both ISO 9001:2015 (Quality Management System) and ISO 14001:2015 (Environmental Management System). This was not a classroom simulation — it produced a structured findings report and corrective action recommendations using the same methodology applied in practice at Royal Enfield and Atmus Filtration.

---

## What I Did

**Audit Planning**
- Defined audit scope, objectives, and criteria aligned with ISO 9001:2015 and ISO 14001:2015 requirements
- Developed audit checklists covering process-based audit methodology
- Scheduled and structured audit activities across functional process areas

**Audit Execution**
- Conducted process-based interviews and document reviews against standard requirements
- Evaluated QMS and EMS effectiveness against defined performance criteria
- Identified non-conformances at both major and minor severity levels
- Documented objective evidence for all findings

**Findings & Reporting**
- Documented all non-conformances with root cause context and supporting evidence
- Developed corrective action recommendations (CAR) for each finding
- Produced a structured audit findings report meeting ISO internal audit documentation standards

---

## Result

> 📋 Full cycle: planning → execution → non-conformance identification → CAR documentation

---

## Connection to Professional Work

The methodology used here is identical to what was applied during on-site supplier audits at **Royal Enfield Global HQ** (Jan 2023) and **Atmus Filtration Technologies** (Jan–Dec 2025) — both involving ISO 9001:2015 evaluation of Tier 1 and Tier 2 suppliers. This project provided the academic foundation; the professional roles applied it at scale.
