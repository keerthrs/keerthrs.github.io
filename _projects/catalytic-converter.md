# Hybrid Vehicle Catalytic Converter

**Category:** Thermal & Emissions Engineering
**Outcome:** Met full emission standards with integrated EHC technology

---

## Overview

Designed a catalytic converter with integrated Electric Heating Catalyst (EHC) technology for a hybrid vehicle application. The project covered thermal and flow engineering analysis, metal component design, material selection, and manufacturability optimization.

---

## What I Did

**Design & Analysis**
- Designed metal converter components and housing in CAD with full dimensional specifications
- Performed thermal analysis to model heat generation, transfer, and distribution across operating conditions
- Conducted flow engineering analysis to optimize exhaust gas residence time and catalyst contact
- Integrated EHC technology to enable fast catalyst light-off from cold start — a primary emission challenge in hybrid drivetrains

**Material Selection & DFM**
- Selected substrate and housing materials meeting thermal, chemical, and structural performance requirements
- Optimized design for manufacturability and cost-effective production at scale
- Evaluated coating and catalyst material options against emission performance targets

**Validation**
- Confirmed design compliance with emission performance standards through analytical modeling
